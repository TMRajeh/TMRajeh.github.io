About Me

I am a Senior Lecturer and Associate Research Fellow at Stirling College, Chengdu University, with a Ph.D. in Computer Science from Southwest Jiaotong University. My research spans reinforcement learning, urban computing, and computer vision. I have extensive teaching experience across UK-Chinese and Middle Eastern institutions and have published in leading journals such as IEEE Transactions on Intelligent Transportation Systems and Knowledge-Based Systems. I actively contribute to the AI community as a reviewer, session chair, and invited speaker, while advancing new work on vision-based reinforcement learning, lifelong learning, and LLM-RL integration.
